Instructions
To use this, first click "Make new link". You will be redirected to Finverse's linking session.
From there, you can select a bank to link with, enter your credentials, and wait till it reaches a success state.
After that, click continue, you will return to this app. Then click the "Check link status" button every now and then, till the server finished processing your account and you see the "Download" button.
